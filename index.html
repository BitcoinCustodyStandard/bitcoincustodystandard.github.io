BCS Normative Summary

This publication defines the normative baseline for resilient Bitcoin custody architectures, and describes how resilience can be measured and communicated via headline metrics including the Bitcoin Custody Resilience Index (BCRI), Entropy Risk Index (ERI), and Exposure Risk Index (XRI).
